    ABOUT


Joe's Pizza first set up on Mars in the year 2087, ten years after the colonization efforts began. To this day Joe and his team of Martians have been making delicious pizzas using ingredients from both Earth and our new home.


We pride ourselves in using the freshest ingredients from all around the colonies to make sure that you get the highest quality food imaginable - and it won't give you space gas!
